TRADING

Scalping

Specializes in profiting even with small price changes. It can be done several times in a day.

Day Trading

Form of trade involves buying and selling stocks in a single day and requires proficiency in market matters and a good understanding of market volatility.

Swing Trading

Used to capitalize on short-term stock patterns and to earn gains from stock within a few days of purchasing it.

About Trading

Trading is one of the most profitable activities on the Internet. Every day, billions are earned and lost at the click of a mouse by people who trade online. As a trader, you trade in financial instruments such as stocks, currency pairs, and index funds.
